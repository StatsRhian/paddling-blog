A short peer paddle before our SKUK adventures course tomorrow. One of the calmest days I've ever seen on Anglesey. Almost no swell at all.

The air and the water felt warm for October. 
Did a little loop around Rhoscolyn beacon but no movement at all. We had to keep a distance there were seals and it's pupping season. 

After the paddle, I drove over to South Stack for sunset.


With Paul, Sarah, Mark, Kev, Cath, Claire, Josh, Will
