- [Strava](https://www.strava.com/activities/15278552368)

Skills practice with Kev and Mark. 

Sarah and Paul had set off early to do SKA training. They rang to let us know it was pretty spicy off the pier.

Although it was very calm in between the piers, as we rounded the north pier there was a tide race. We also had a big ship come out, beeping at two sit on top kayakers who were sitting in the channel. 

When we got to Cullercoats I tried and failed a roll. Kev got out and helped me practise whilst Mark gave advice. Eventually, after opening my eyes and watching the paddle blade I managed 3 successful rolls.  
Thanks Mark & Kev 💙
