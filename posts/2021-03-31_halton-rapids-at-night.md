Dusk & night paddle. 13 paddlers in total. Halton on medium (0.85m). Ran the rapids once stopping at the islands to play. The others then did 2 more laps in the dark whilst I played at the islands. Paul brought glowsticks for everyone and hot cross buns. Lovely way to close the season.
